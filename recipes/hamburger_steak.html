Saucy Hamburger Steaks Recipe:

These juicy Hamburger Steaks are a family favorite! Made with simple pantry ingredients, this quick and easy recipe delivers big flavor and is perfect for busy weeknights when you need a hearty, home-cooked meal fast. Here are the following ingridients that you'll need to make this classic recipe.

  • 1 1/2 pounds of ground beef
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt and black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of ground mustard
  • 1 small onion diced thinly
  • 1 packed of brown gravy mix
  • 1 can of French onion condensed soup (undiluted)
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1 tablespoon of Worcestershire source
  1. Crumble the ground beef into a large bowl and sprinkle with garlic powder, onion powder, salt, pepper, and the ground mustard. Mix everything together well.
  2. Divide the mixture into equal parts and form into sizable patties.
  3. Place a skillet over medium-high heat. Once the pan is hot, place the steaks in the skillet, cooking for around 4 minutes without touching.
  4. Flip the patties over and follow the same process. Once the 4 minutes are over, remove the patties from the skillet pan and rest them on paper towels.
  5. Drain away until about 2 teaspoons of the leftover grease of the pan. Apply the pan back to the heat and add the sliced onions to caramelize them. Cook for 3 minutes and then add the onion soup, gravy mix, and water. Stir well.
  6. Bring to boil, reduce the heat to a simmer, and then stir in the remaining Worcestershire sauce. Add the steaks back to the pan and finish cooking for about 5 minutes or until they are done to your liking.
