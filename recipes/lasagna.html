Easy 1 Hour 40 Minute Lasagna Recipe:

This easy recipe for a classic American Lasagna is great for the family or party guests enough to fill their stomachs! Here are the following ingridients needed to make this tasty dish:

  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Beef Bouillon
  • Dried Parsley
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Garlic Salt
  • Eggs
  • Sour Cream
  • Lasagna Noodle (1 Pack)
  • Grated Parmesan

The following steps to create this traditional dish goes as follows:

  1. Prepare the meat sauce by cooking the beef and chopped onion over medium heat until soft and no longer pink. Don't forget to drain any excess fat from the pan. Add some water, the tomato sauce, Bouillon granules, and additional seasonings. Bring the mixture to a boil and then reduce the heat. additional, reduce the heat and simmer the sauce for half an hour.
  2. We begin assembling the lasagna by mixing our remaining ingridients. In a small bowl of choice, mix the eggs completely with the cheese and sour cream. Measured at a half cup, spread the finished meat sauce into a greased 13x9-inch baking dish. Layer it with 4 of the cooked lasagna noodles, the cottage cheese mixture and the pepperoni. Top the additional layer with the remaining noodles and meat sauce layer by layer. Once completely covered, sprinkle some shredded mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses evenly over the top.
  3. Bake the now constucted lasagna into the oven by covering it and baking at a 350F for 35 minutes. Once completed, remove the dish and let it cool for 15 minutes before cutting and serving.
