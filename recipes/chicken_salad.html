Classic Chicken Salad Recipe:

Here's a great recipe to use with any leftover chicken that you may have after cooking a different dish to create something refreshing and bold for your next lunch session, whether it be at work or at home for a quick bite! Here are the following ingridients needed to make this tasty chicken salad:

  • 2 cups of any type of chicken that is pre-cooked chopped or shredded
  • 1 thinnly diced celery
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1/2 cup of mayonnaise
  • Almonds, Walnuts, or Pecans
  • 1 teaspoon of chopped fresh dill or dried
  • Sliced cucumbers and roma tomatoes

Once gathered our list of ingridients, we follow these simple instructions to mix them all together.

  1. Chop the cooked and chilled chicken into various pieces of size into a large washable bowl.
  2. Depending on the choosed vegetables, wash them before chopping them into thin slices or bits into the bowl with the chicken.
  3. Slowly add in the mayonnaise while mixing all the contents of the bowl together Add additional salt and pepper if desired. If consistency is too thin as desired, add more mayonnaise per spoon intervals into the bowl.
